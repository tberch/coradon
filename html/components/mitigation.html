Radon Mitigation Systems

Our certified technicians install custom-designed radon mitigation systems to effectively reduce radon levels in your home. All systems installed according to EPA protocols and ANSI/AARST standards.

Sub-Slab Depressurization

The most common and effective system, creating a negative pressure zone under your foundation to collect radon before it enters your home and venting it safely outside.

Sump Pit Systems

For homes with sump pumps, we install specialized sealed covers and piping systems that prevent radon from entering through your sump pit while maintaining pump functionality.

New Construction

Passive or active radon-resistant features installed during construction, meeting or exceeding Colorado building code requirements at a fraction of the cost of post-construction mitigation.

System Maintenance

Regular inspections and maintenance to ensure your system continues to operate at peak efficiency. We service all systems, even those installed by other companies.
