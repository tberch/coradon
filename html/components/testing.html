Professional Radon Testing

Testing is the only way to know if your home has elevated radon levels. We offer state-of-the-art testing services to accurately determine your home's radon concentration. All tests performed by certified professionals.

Short-Term Testing

48-hour tests using calibrated continuous radon monitors. Perfect for real estate transactions and quick assessments. Results available immediately after the test period.

Long-Term Testing

90+ day tests using alpha track detectors for the most accurate assessment of your annual average radon exposure. Recommended for occupied homes not in real estate transactions.

Real Estate Testing

Specialized testing for home buyers and sellers. Fast turnaround and detailed reports that satisfy all Colorado disclosure requirements and home inspector recommendations.

Post-Mitigation Testing
